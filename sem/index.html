Software Engineering Management !

In the course of Software Enginnering and Management @ BITS Pilani, the class was divided into four teams namely,

  1. Li8 Technologies
  2. D`Falcons
  3. NDAARP
  4. Cube-X

Each team had to propose a product idea, design and develop the product following all the principles of software engineering starting from software model, cost estimation to software testing and documentation.

Li8 Technologies proposed the idea of a centralized Notice board for the students of BITS Pilani. NLite Web Admin and NLite Mobile App

D'Falcons proposed a college news aggregator to keep track of various technical and cultural events across premier colleges. College Khabar

NDAARP proposed a personal assistace mobile app with smart priority system. Your Assist

Cube-X proposed an app that would connect student and professors of BITS Pilani along with providing basic information about BITS Pilani. Stay On Time
